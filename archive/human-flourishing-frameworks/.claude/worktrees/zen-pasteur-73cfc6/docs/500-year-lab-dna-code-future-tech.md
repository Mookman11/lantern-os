# 500-Year Lab DNA-Code / Future-Tech Ladder

Status: draft lab note, docs-only.
Last reviewed: 2026-05-13.

## Purpose

This is the missing second 500-year lab table: DNA-code treatments and future
biotech only, scored on a 0-100 confidence scale.

This is not the prevention-first healthspan table. It deliberately excludes
food, sleep, exercise, vaccines, routine screening, and ordinary standard care
unless they are part of a DNA-code or future-tech lane.

## Hard boundary

This file is not medical advice and does not authorize treatment.

It does not provide:

```text
gene sequences
vectors
doses
injection routes
lab protocols
DIY biology instructions
clinic recommendations
self-experimentation plans
immortality claims
```

All real use belongs to qualified clinicians, regulated trials, approved products,
formal ethics review, and current law.

## Score meaning

```text
100 = proven general 500-year human treatment
80-99 = approved or strongly validated for specific diseases, not general longevity
60-79 = real clinical lane, early approvals or strong disease-specific plausibility
40-59 = early human trials / strong preclinical platform, not proven for longevity
20-39 = speculative but coherent future geroscience
1-19 = extremely speculative or blocked outside trials
0 = false claim / impossible with current evidence
```

## WWIII / civilization-risk note

World War III is not a treatment. It belongs in the external hardening / falsifier
layer: war, nuclear escalation, supply-chain collapse, institutional collapse,
and loss of labs can destroy the path even if the science improves.

Therefore the 500-year lab has two parallel requirements:

```text
1. biological code repair and future medicine
2. civilization continuity, peace, redundancy, and ethical governance
```
