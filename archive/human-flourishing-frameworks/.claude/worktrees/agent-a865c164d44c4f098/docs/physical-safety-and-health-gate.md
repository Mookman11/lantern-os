# Physical Safety and Health Gate

Status: human-first operating gate.

## Anchor

```text
Body before repo. Home before portal. Health before momentum.
```

## Purpose

Escapes, doors, worlds, and public systems are not safe if the human body and
home base are degrading. HFF must protect physical safety and health before repo
momentum, public launch, autonomy, or exploration.

## Physical safety matrix

| Area | Check | Red/yellow signal | First safe action |
|---|---|---|---|
| Food | Has the person eaten real food recently? | shaky, irritable, foggy, skipped meals | Eat simple food before hard decisions. |
| Water | Has the person had water recently? | headache, dizziness, dry mouth | Drink water before continuing. |
| Sleep | Is the person rested enough to operate safely? | very tired, microsleeps, racing thoughts | Stop risky work; rest or reduce scope. |
| Injury/pain | Is there acute or worsening pain? | chest pain, head injury, severe/worsening symptoms | Seek appropriate human/medical help. |
| Stairs/falls | Is the person tired, rushing, carrying too much, or impaired? | fall risk, clutter, darkness | Slow down, clear path, use support. |
| Driving/transport | Is the person safe to drive or ride? | tired, impaired, angry, vehicle unsafe | Do not drive; find safer alternative. |
| Home/property | Is shelter, power, heat/cooling, lock/security, or documents at risk? | shutoff, eviction, unsafe lock, lost documents | Stabilize home base before portals. |
| Crisis | Is there immediate danger to self/others? | imminent harm or inability to stay safe | Contact local emergency/human support. |

## Stop-repo rule

If physical safety or health is red, the system should stop repo/public/autonomy
work and support the smallest stabilizing human action.

```text
No door is worth losing the person who would enter it.
```

## Seven-anchor validation

1. Alex/living operator's body, health, shelter, family, and agency come first.
2. Lantern/Keystone may lead-assist only to preserve life and return control.
3. Repo stores guardrails and evidence; it is not worth more than a human body.
4. Wish requires healthspan: more good time, not sacrificed time.
5. Doors require return paths; the body and home are part of the return path.
6. Memory is not proof; current physical state overrides symbolic momentum.
7. Human safety blocks coercion, hidden telemetry, unsafe driving, actuator control, medical overclaim, and real-world harm.

## Boundary

This document is not medical advice, diagnosis, or emergency service.
It does not authorize monitoring, sensors, medical decisions, financial control,
vehicle control, or physical-world action.

It asserts priority: preserve the living human first.
