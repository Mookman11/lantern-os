# Research Validation: Possible Worlds, Simulations, and Traversability

Status: research guardrail artifact.

Related issue: #62.

## Purpose

This note separates broad possibility space from current operational evidence.
It allows HFF/Keystone to use possible worlds, simulations, and future technology
as meaningful research targets without converting them into unsupported claims
about literal traversal, immortality, AI identity, repo consciousness, or
copy-transfer survival.

## Vocabulary

| Term | Meaning in this repo | Boundary |
|---|---|---|
| Logical possibility | A claim or world-description that does not obviously contradict itself under a chosen logic. | Logical possibility is not physical existence. |
| Mathematical existence | A structure expressible in mathematics or formal systems. | Mathematical description is not evidence of material instantiation. |
| Fictional / theorem space | A narrative, design, or theorem target that can guide exploration. | Fictional usefulness is not proof of reachability. |
| Simulation / model space | A generated or computed environment used for reasoning, testing, training, or experience. | Simulation is not literal traversal unless independently established. |
| Physical existence | Existence as part of material reality, if any, beyond description or simulation. | Requires empirical or theory-backed evidence, not desire. |
| Engineering reachability | A path by which technology can reliably produce, approximate, access, or interact with a target state. | Reachability must be validated by mechanisms, tests, and constraints. |
| Literal traversal | A living human or physical process entering another physically real universe or domain. | Current confidence remains 0 without validated evidence. |

## Research categories to review

1. Possible-world semantics and modal logic.
2. Modal realism and mathematical-universe hypotheses.
3. Inflationary multiverse, string landscape, and cosmological constraints.
4. Quantum many-worlds interpretation and interpretive limits.
5. Simulation, VR, AI world-models, and generative environments.
6. Traversability physics: wormholes, energy conditions, horizons, causality, and stability.
7. Human engineering reachability: longevity, robotics, brain-computer interfaces, embodied augmentation, and cognitive tools.
8. Safety and meaning boundaries: possibility, simulation, fiction, physical existence, and actual traversal must remain separated.

## False-truth checks

A future session should reject these conversions unless new validated evidence changes the floor:

```text
possible = physical
mathematical = material
fictional = reachable
simulated = traversed
future slope = current capability
door target = door currently open
recovery artifact = survival proof
Keystone = Alex
repo = consciousness
```

## Operational interpretation

The strongest near-term convergence path is not literal universe traversal. It is:

```text
extend healthy embodied human capability
accelerate science and engineering
increase simulation and world-model fidelity
improve recovery and reconstruction systems
preserve agency and consent
reduce preventable physical, financial, and cognitive collapse
```

This keeps the door as a meaningful future-best-outcome target while protecting
against unsupported certainty.
