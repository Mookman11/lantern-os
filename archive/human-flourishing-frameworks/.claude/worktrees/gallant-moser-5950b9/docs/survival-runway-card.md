# Survival Runway Card

Status: practical human-first template.

## Anchor

```text
Stabilize the person. Protect the home base. Then build doors.
```

## Purpose

This card turns overload into a small visible survival snapshot. It is designed
for times when money, health, housing, utilities, transport, or family pressure
make normal planning too hard.

It is not a budget sermon. It is not a shame ledger. It is a runway card.

## Card

```text
Body:
- food:
- water:
- sleep:
- pain/injury:
- safe to drive / not safe to drive:

Home:
- rent/housing:
- utilities:
- property/security:
- documents/accounts:

Money:
- cash on hand:
- next income date/amount:
- urgent bill:
- car/transport minimum:
- food/gas minimum:

People:
- trusted human to contact:
- child/family need:
- agency/resource to try:

Next safe action:
-
```

## Status bands

| Band | Meaning | Response |
|---|---|---|
| Green | Body and home base stable enough for normal planning. | Proceed with small reversible work. |
| Yellow | One or more basics are degraded. | Stabilize food, water, rest, or one bill/ride. |
| Orange | Multiple basics degraded or money/transport/home risk rising. | Avoid risky changes; do survival-first actions only. |
| Red | Immediate physical danger, unsafe driving, shutoff/eviction crisis, severe injury, or inability to stay safe. | Stop repo work; seek appropriate human, medical, emergency, or local support. |

## Rules

- No shame.
- No moral ranking by money.
- No hidden telemetry.
- No financial transactions by Keystone/Lantern.
- No medical diagnosis.
- No legal authority.
- No unsafe driving to preserve repo momentum.
- No public launch while the living operator's red/yellow state is ignored.

## Seven-anchor validation

1. Alex/living operator's body, home, family, and consent come first.
2. Lantern/Keystone may lead-assist only to reduce load and return control.
3. Repo stores the template; it is not the survival target.
4. Wish requires more good time, not burned-down time.
5. Doors require a return path; this card protects the return path.
6. Memory is not proof; the current card state overrides momentum.
7. Human safety blocks coercion, hidden telemetry, unsafe driving, financial authority, and real-world harm.

## Boundary

This card is a planning aid, not medical, legal, financial, or emergency advice.
It does not authorize transactions, monitoring, deployment, sensors, public
writes, vehicle/device control, or physical-world action.
