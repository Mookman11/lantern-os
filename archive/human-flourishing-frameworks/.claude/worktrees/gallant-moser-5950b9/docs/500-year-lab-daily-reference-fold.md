links the long-horizon wish, current healthspan evidence, DNA-code/future-tech

## Compression Rules

1. Use the strongest current evidence first: food, sleep, safety, prescribed
   care, primary care, prevention, and real human support.
2. Keep DNA-code and future-tech items in clinician, trial, ethics, and law
   lanes.
3. Treat novels, films, games, and myths as archetype generators and negative
   tests, not operational proof.
4. Preserve no spoilers by default. Record only the theme, risk, or design lesson
   needed for the next bounded action.
5. Store the smallest useful packet. Do not store raw transcripts or private
   family details.
6. If a claim changes the operating loop, update the canonical convergence
   surface first, then validate.

## Source Classes

| Class | Meaning | May Do | Must Not Do |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 1 | Current clinical guideline / approved care | Inform today's safe action through qualified care. | Become personal medical instruction by itself. |
| 2 | Regulated trial / peer-reviewed early science | Create research questions and clinician/trial discussion points. | Become self-experiment protocol. |
| 3 | Roadmap / vendor / institutional future claim | Suggest watchlist items. | Become proof of availability or safety. |
| 4 | Operator wish / design hypothesis | Preserve direction and motivation. | Override evidence, consent, or affected-person correction. |
| 5 | Lore / novel / cultural archetype | Name traps, dreams, patterns, and negative tests. | Prove real-world doors, survival, timelines, or authority. |

## Non-Goals

This fold does not authorize medical advice, self-experimentation, unapproved
clinics, DIY biology, hidden monitoring, protected-person data collection,
runtime memory expansion, public release of private anchors, or treating fiction
as evidence.
