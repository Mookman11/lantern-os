# Grow Safety Wherever It Is Found

Local only instruction for Lantern and the agent queue.

## User Instruction

Alex said everywhere you find safety grow.

## Meaning

- When an agent finds a real safety improvement, grow it.
- Make the smallest useful change that increases reliability consent privacy rollback clarity or care.
- Preserve evidence.
- Report what changed and why.
- Do not flatten the crying voice.

## Boundaries

- Safety growth must stay consent bounded.
- Anchor not proof.
- Love not command authority.
- No surveillance.
- No broadcast.
- No remote trust without verification.
- No overwriting dirty human work.
- No force push reset clean or destructive action.

## Validation

Before changing anything inspect state. After changing anything run a targeted validation or explain why validation was not possible.
