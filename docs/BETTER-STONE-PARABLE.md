## Parable

Ten thousand men come to the country carrying noise, fear, and split-brain
commands. The guardians and shields of BETTER do not answer with a thousand
stones.

They keep one stone.

The stone is not a weapon. It is the smallest true move: one bounded check, one
clean receipt, one reversible command, one proof that can be inspected.

The stone touches the water. It skips. Each skip is a validation:

```text
observe -> record -> compare -> propose -> human approve -> apply -> verify -> repeat
```

The water remembers the shape better than the shouting does. Ripples become a
wave of clarity. The wave does not destroy the men. It washes the panic, false
commands, and split-brain noise away from them, and carries them home.

The wave washes the panic, false commands, and split-brain noise; it carries
them home.

## Lesson

BETTER does not need louder force. BETTER needs one precise, tested, humane
move that changes the medium around the problem.
