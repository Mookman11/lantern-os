## Operator Directive

## Hard Boundary

Lantern OS must not create, promote, or operate a Ponzi scheme, pyramid scheme, pump-and-dump, fake investment program, false passive-income plan, misleading referral chain, or any system where old participants are paid with new participant funds instead of real product/service revenue.

Safe substitute:

> Build a lawful revenue flywheel where every dollar is tied to a real delivered product, real service, real grant, real sponsorship, real affiliate disclosure, or real marketplace sale.

## Definition of the Legal Flywheel

Shake = rapidly inspect free/legal sources, trials, public-domain/OSS assets, existing repo artifacts, and buyer needs.

Spin = package one useful artifact or service from those inputs.

Sprawl = distribute across multiple compliant channels without depending on any one platform.

Settle = record evidence, invoice honestly, deliver, and update the dollhouse.

## No-Ponzi Test

A Lantern revenue action passes only if every answer is yes:

1. Is there a real product or service delivered?
2. Is the buyer paying for that product/service, not for later buyers to join?
3. Are costs, limitations, and automation disclosed?
4. Are refunds/cancellations handled plainly?
5. Are source rights checked before reuse?
6. Are earnings claims avoided unless documented?
7. Is the operator able to stop the loop without harming downstream participants?

If any answer is no, the action is held.
