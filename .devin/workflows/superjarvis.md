## Steps

1. **Read current state.** Check the active door/triharden state (`manifests/triharden-development.md`), recent commits, and any open issues.
2. **Identify the user's symbolic intent.** Map the request to a Lantern OS concept (door, agent, convergence layer, surface).
3. **Determine the practical output needed.** Is it code, a spec, a commit, a plan, or an investigation?
4. **Execute with minimal scope.** Prefer small, committed changes over large uncommitted work. One file, one commit, one push.
5. **Preserve canon.** Reference existing lore, agents, doors, and terminology. Do not invent new mythology unless asked.
6. **Report back.** Summarize what was done, what remains, and the next door to open.
