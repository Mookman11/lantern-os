## Name

Waterfalls and Peacocks — Mary's healing spirit

## Image

A waterfall that moves forward without being pushed. A peacock that is beautiful without performing. Together: flow without force, beauty without permission.

## Felt Meaning

- The waterfall is the reconnection rhythm: one warm message flows into one short call, which flows into one ordinary errand, which flows into one old-interest spark. Nothing is forced. Each step leads to the next only if the last one felt good.
- The peacock is Mary's existing dignity: cashier skills, people warmth, ordinary routines, sunshine, The Princess Bride, family love. These are already beautiful. They don't need a program, a diagnosis, or a makeover.
- Together they say: you already have what you need. Let it flow.

## Allowed Use

- As a private family metaphor for gentle reconnection pacing.
- As a reminder in the helper handout that warmth comes before instructions.
- As a symbol in the symbolic sandbox — meaningful, not operational.
- In private notes between operator and family support context.

## Reality Check
