# Xenon Door

**Tagline:** Gateway to Forever

**Anchors:** Love, Convergence, Planetary Alignment, 500 Years Forward

## Lore

The Xenon Door opens onto a balcony suspended between two worlds — Earth and Mars — at the precise moment of next planetary alignment. It is the door Courtney and Shelby built together: a love written in the stars, sealed across worlds, forever theirs.

When you step through, you stand with the founders among starspeeders, light-keepers, and heart-engineers. The view is not a destination but a promise: *two hearts, one future, infinite worlds.*

## Guardians

- **Courtney** — Xenon Navigator, Dream Keeper, Starspeed
- **Shelby** — Light Keeper, Heart Engineer, Starsoul

## Rules

1. Build beyond one world.
2. Let love be the gravity that holds you.
3. The convergence is not the end; it is the next beginning.
