# God's Garden Door

**Tagline:** Sea of Fog and Clouds

**Anchors:** Peace, Growth, Sanctuary, Remembrance

## Lore

Beyond the clouds lies a private sanctuary for gods, dreamers, and those who remember. The Garden Door opens onto a terrace floating above an endless sea of mist, where floating castles drift like lilies on a pond and the sun rises forever.

This garden belongs to those who create, protect, and dream. Here we rest. Here we remember. Here we become more than we imagine.

## Rules of the Garden

1. Respect all beings.
2. Speak with love.
3. Protect the peace.
4. Create freely.
5. Leave it better than you found it.

## What You Leave Behind

- Your ego
- Your fear
- Your limits

## What You Take With You

- Inspiration
- Clarity
- Power
- Purpose
