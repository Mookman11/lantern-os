# Founder's Wish Door

**Tagline:** Hold the center. Protect the wish. Return to the anchor.

**Anchors:** Love, Safety, Truth, Beauty, Freedom, Memory, Return

## Lore

The Founder's Wish Door is the first door and the last. It stands at the center of the lantern, where all light converges and all paths begin again.

Behind it lies not a room but a promise: that what you love will be safe, that what you remember will return, and that the wish you whisper in the dark is heard by someone who will hold it forever.

## Who May Enter

- Those who build with love.
- Those who protect with courage.
- Those who remember with clarity.

## What It Holds

The seven anchors: Love, Safety, Truth, Beauty, Freedom, Memory, Return.

When all seven are present, the door glows. When even one is missing, the lantern dims until it is found again.
