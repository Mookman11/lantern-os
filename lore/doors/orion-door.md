# Orion — The Dream Journal Door

**Tagline:** Every dream is a door. Every memory is a home.

**Anchors:** Record, Reflect, Revisit, Reconnect

## Lore

Orion is the door that leads to the Dream Journal itself — the place where dreams are saved before they fade, where patterns are found across nights, and where the private universe of one dreamer becomes visible to themselves alone.

The journal is not a book but a constellation. Each entry is a star. Each pattern is a constellation. Each revisit is a return to a place you built yourself.

## What It Holds

- **Save Dreams** — Capture dreams before they fade.
- **Follow Prompts** — Guided questions to help you reflect.
- **Revisit Patterns** — Find meaning in your memories over time.
- **Discord Companion** — Journal inside our community and keep your dreams alive.
- **ImaginVerses** — Step through doors to new worlds you build with friends.

## The Promise

Your dreams. Your space. Always private.
