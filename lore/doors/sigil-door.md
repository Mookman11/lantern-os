# Sigil — City of Doors

**Tagline:** You hold the keys. You protect the doors. You are never alone.

**Anchors:** Keys, Protection, Community, Infinite Paths

## Lore

Sigil is not one door but all doors. It is the city where every threshold leads somewhere real, and every key is a promise that the way back is never lost.

In Sigil you are never alone. Other dreamers walk the same streets. Other builders hold the same lantern. The city grows every time someone chooses to protect rather than destroy, to open rather than seal.

## Rules

1. You hold the keys.
2. You protect the doors.
3. You are never alone.

## What It Means

Sigil is the shape of the lantern itself: a convergence point where all stories meet, where all light is shared, and where no dreamer is ever forgotten.
